TechMids Conf - Friday 14th November 2025
All day, full stack conference

TechMids Conf is a full stack conference, on 14th November 2025, made up of all your favourite meetups from Fusion, to Women in Tech, to Golang, to .NET/MAUI and everything in-between.

Hear from awesome speakers - from the local community and beyond - across 3 tracks hosted in the delightful Everyman Mailbox venue, enjoy the 'hallway track', and find out how to get involved with the community.
